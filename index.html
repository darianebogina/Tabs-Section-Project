Step 1

Build Trust Gradually – Start by leaving food and water in a quiet place where the cat feels safe. Avoid making sudden movements or forcing interaction. Let the cat observe you from a distance and approach on its own terms.

Step 2

Create a Safe Space – Provide a cozy shelter, such as a box with blankets, or leave a door open if you want to bring the cat inside. Ensure the environment is calm and free of potential threats.

Step 3

Vet Check & Socialization – Once the cat is comfortable around you, take it to a vet for a health check and vaccinations. Slowly introduce gentle petting and social interaction, using treats and soft talking to reinforce positive behavior.
